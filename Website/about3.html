ASKO Engineering LTD was established on June 2002, as one of the second addition to the ASKO Group of Companies. The responsibility of the company is to act as market leader for supply, installation, testing, and commissioning of air conditioner for home and commercial, generator, substation, projector and other audiovisual and training equipments. The company is enlisted in different government organizations and also have ABC supervisory license from electrical board which is the legal authority of government of Bangladesh to issue such types of license.

Our work is our pride, and at ASKO Engineering LTD, we ensure that our consumers get the best use of their investment. The company strives to continue its ever growing quality and encourages equal effort to all the other parts of the company.
